Welcome to EasyRoom

Having roommates and housemates is a growing actuality of young adult life, and can be a great or mortifying experience. Our goal with EasyRoom is to provide a remedy for the latter, to allow people living together to have a more convenient way to communicate.

This site includes features like reminders, schedules, and other handy ways to communicate amongst roommates. Set up a chores list and rotation to remind housemates of their duties. EasyRoom is a way for roommates and housemates alike to live together more harmoniously.
